eCrowne provides <AffiliateLink type={ProductType.ESP32} /> and <AffiliateLink type={ProductType.ESP8266} /> firmware support for SimHub and other projects, enabling sim racers to create custom hardware devices for their racing setup with the power of ESP devices.

## What is eCrowne?

eCrowne develops and maintains firmware that allows <AffiliateLink type={ProductType.ESP32} /> and <AffiliateLink type={ProductType.ESP8266} /> microcontrollers to communicate with SimHub, a popular sim racing telemetry and control software; among other things.
